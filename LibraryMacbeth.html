Overview

In the story 'Macbeth' written by William Shakespeare, several techniques are incorporated to set the storyline through Act 1 Scene 1. While impacting the audience through creating the background and overview of the situation for the story, the readers will be greatly affected by the plot of the story. Through the ominous setting, sinister mood, and use of wordplay, this scene of Macbeth can be impactful for the audience while setting the basis of the story.
