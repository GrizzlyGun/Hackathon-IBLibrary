About me.

Hi I'm Saumya, a 18 year old Economics student. While I find that I have a sort of gravitational pull towards economics, politics, and their works, be rest assured that my interests aren't only limited to them!


In the fine and music arts, I revel in my creative and artistic freedom. Through public speaking I find a voice and indulge in my confidence, while my writing gives me another avenue to channel my thoughts and opinions. Through the cultural differences that make up my environment, I am constantly motivated to bring the same diversity into my own development.


While most of my posts do seem to be strictly economics based, I am trying to branch out into my different fields of interest to find more things to write about. I hope you enjoy reading them and I look forward to interacting with you and receiving your feedback!
